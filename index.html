About

I am an Arab American, born in the United States, but I spent most of my life in Lebanon. I returned to the USA for college, eager to start a new chapter. With determination, I secured admission to a reputable school with financial aid. To cover my remaining tuition and living expenses, I worked tirelessly, holding various blue-collar jobs in retail, sales, customer service, and even a full-time role as a security operations officer for my university's police department, all before turning 20.

Through my hard work, I financed my education and supported my family. Now, I am committed to investing in myself and pursuing my aspirations of becoming a creator, provider, and leader. I aim to contribute to the society that has given me so much, ensuring the cycle of prosperity continues.
